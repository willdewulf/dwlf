# DWLF – Project Context

## Artist

**Name:** DWLF  
**Origin:** Belgian-born, Stockholm-based  
**Genre:** House & Techno

### Sound & Influences

Big bumpy kicks, lush warm pads, and intricate FM synthesis. Inspired by classic techno and forward-thinking artists like JakoJako, Christian AB, Objekt, and Ben UFO.

### Background

Started recording Eurotrance from Belgian radio onto cassettes at age 5. Seven years later, began producing with a cracked copy of Fruity Loops. Today, music is made and performed on a hardware-only setup — the constraint brings the most joy.

Sets are performed live: prepared patterns played through controllers, elements looped on the fly, and DJ-mixed into the next track. Melodies and patterns get recycled and reassembled per gig — the collection is a fluid, shifting body of music.

No fixed goal. Equal joy in dancing alone in a room as playing live to a crowd. Music is a never-ending search for a personal sound, built from experiences and memories, sequenced into something that moves.
